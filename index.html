This SERVICE AGREEMENT is entered into March 1, 2017 (the “Effective Date”) between:

AVENGERS SOLUTIONS INC.,

a corporation with offices at
Suite 1002, 10 Dundas Street East, Toronto, Ontario, Canada, M5B 2K3

and

HI-FI HOVERBOARDS INC.,

827 Dundas Street West, Toronto, Ontario, M6J 1V4
(“Hi-Fi Hoverboards”).

OVERVIEW

  1. Avengers Solutions wishes to hire Hi-Fi Hoverboards to provide consulting and design services related to the branding of its new software platform.
  2. Hi-Fi Hoverboards agrees to provide such services to Avengers Solutions on the terms of this Agreement.

In consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

  1. DEFINITIONS

    1. “Business Day" means any day other than a Saturday, Sunday or statutory holiday observed in the Province of Ontario.
    2. "Confidential Information" means all confidential, scientific, financial, and other information; all manufacturing, marketing, sales and distribution data; all client and vendor information, including identifying information; all scientific and test data, documents, methods, techniques, formulations, operations, know-how, experience, skill, trade secrets, secret processes, practices, ideas, inventions, designs, devices, reports, plans and drawings disclosed by one party to another party, whether orally or in writing, and identified by the disclosing party as being confidential.
    3. “Intellectual Property” means all information, data, designs, processes, works and inventions, including those that are or may be the subject of patent, copyright, industrial design, trademark or other forms of protection.

  2. OPERATIVE PROVISIONS

    1. Currency. All references to dollar amounts in this Agreement shall be in the lawful currency of Canada.
    2. Assignment. Either party may assign any or all of its rights or obligations under this Agreement to another Person on thirty (30) days’ notice to the other party, and the other party shall have no right to object to such an assignment.

  3. SERVICES AND BUDGET

    1. Hi-Fi Hoverboards shall provide Avengers Solutions with consulting and design services as determined through mutual discussions by the parties.
    2. All services provided by Hi-Fi Hoverboards pursuant to this Agreement shall be provided between March 1 and May 31, 2017. Hi-Fi Hoverboards shall periodically invoice Avengers Solutions for services completed during this period.
    3. The total budget for this project is $180,600.00 (exclusive of HST). Hi-Fi Hoverboards shall not invoice, and Avengers Solutions shall not be required to pay, for any services provided outside of this budget.

  4. TERMINATION

    1. Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing the other with notice of termination in writing.

  5. USE OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

    1. Avengers Solutions shall throughout the entire term of this Agreement and at all times thereafter retain all right, title and interest in its Intellectual Property, in whatever form, including Intellectual Property delivered to Hi-Fi Hoverboards.
    2. Hi-Fi Hoverboards hereby grants to Avengers Solutions an exclusive, royalty-free, and perpetual licence to use any Intellectual Property created by Hi-Fi Hoverboards or its agents for the purpose of providing services under this Agreement.
    3. Hi-Fi Hoverboards acknowledges that it shall have no claim to ownership or use of Avengers Solutions’s Intellectual Property, or any Intellectual Property created for the use of Avengers Solutions, beyond the term of this Agreement.

  6. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

    1. Both parties understand and acknowledge that, by virtue of this Agreement, they may both receive or become aware of information belonging or relating to the other party, its business, business plans, affairs or activities, which is confidential and/or proprietary to the other party and/or its suppliers and/or customers and in respect of which they are bound by a strict duty of confidence.
    2. In consideration of such Confidential Information being disclosed or otherwise made available to either party for the purposes of the performance of this Agreement, both parties hereby undertake that they will not at any time, either before or after the termination of this Agreement, and either directly or indirectly, disclose, divulge or make unauthorized use of any Confidential Information, except to the extent to which such Confidential Information:

      3. Is publicly known at the time of its disclosure or is being made available to them.

    3. After such disclosure or being made available to them, becomes publicly known otherwise than through a breach of this undertaking.
    4. Is required by law, regulation or order of a competent authority to be disclosed by one of the Parties, provided that, where practicable, the other party is given reasonable advance notice of the intended disclosure in order to bring proceedings to prevent such disclosure.

  7. RELATIONSHIP OF PARTIES

    • Nothing in this contract shall be deemed to constitute a partnership in law between the parties. Each party shall perform its activity independently and no other relationship is intended, including that of employment, joint venture, franchise, or other special relationship.

  8. APPLICABLE LAW

    • This Agreement shall be governed and interpreted in accordance with the laws in force in the Province of Ontario.

  9. AMENDMENTS

    • No amendment to this Agreement shall be valid or binding unless it is in writing and signed by each party.

    • COUNTERPARTS AND ELECTRONIC DELIVERY

      • This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, and each counterpart when taken as a whole with the other executed counterparts shall constitute an original agreement. Delivery of an executed copy or counterpart of this Agreement by electronic transmission shall constitute valid and effective delivery.
